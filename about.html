About

Get to know

Franklin is an AI Software Engineer who focuses on creating business insights and AI solutions through state-of-the-art industrial data science technology and applications.

Introduction

Franklin is part of the Gamma X team at Boston Consulting Group.

Prior to joining BCG, during his internship at Deloitte, Franklin was a part of the team developing an advanced analytics customer engagement platform and deployed a single customer view model for a global leading make-up brand, He also worked as a student data scientist processed and analyzed data with millions of rows using unsupervised learning models to detect anomaly patterns in a finance data warehouse for Morgan Stanley. Apart from industrial-level projects, Franklin also has 3 years of data science related research experience in automated driving, structural health monitoring, and image recognition.

Franklin holds a M.P.S. degree in Information Science from Cornell University, a M.S. degree in Systems Engineering from University of California, Berkeley, and a B.Eng. degree in Civil Engineering from Dalian University of Technology, China. He published 2 journal papers and 1 conference paper while pursuing his Bachelor's and Master's degrees.
