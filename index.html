Introduction

Curiosity is always a driving factor in my life. I enjoy deep thinking and explore real-word problems. Such a character has motivated me to achieve many successes in the academic field during my undergraduate study. What is real amazing when I came to UC Berkeley is that I explored data science - a promising field encouraging my deeper thinking and exploration. I am fascinated by the techniques to manipulate the big data, implement the data mining, analyze, and computing. Using tools like Spark and Hadoop to explore big data or analyzing based on machine learning techniques really help us make better decisions and provide a more convenient and efficient life.
