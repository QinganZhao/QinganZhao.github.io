Introduction

Curiosity has always been a driving factor in my life. I enjoy deep thinking and exploring real-word problems. Such a character has motivated me to achieve many successes in the academic field during my undergraduate study. When I came to UC Berkeley, I felt so lucky that I could continue exploring data science - a promising field encouraging my deeper thinking and exploration. I am fascinated by the techniques to manipulate big data, implement data mining, analyzing, and computing. Data science combined with machine learning techniques really helps us make better decisions and provide a more convenient and efficient life. As a student at Cal, where almost everybody could access the state-of-the-art technology, we are encouraged to solve problems through data-driven decision making. Outside of academics, I also enjoy traveling, reading, exercising, watching movies, playing board games and exploring brand new things.
